Dreamgrove is a website dedicated to druid theorycrafting and guides. We aim to collect the most up-to-date knowledge about druids in World of Warcraft. The website is a part of the Dreamgrove Discord.

The website was created by Vinter, with help from Sune, Sheep, Gamz and other Dreamgrove members.

Content on Dreamgrove is hosted in perpetuity, with permission from the authors under a [Creative Commons Non-Commercial ShareAlike license](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/).
