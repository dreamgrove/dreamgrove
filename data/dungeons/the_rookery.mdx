<BossCard title="Trash">
- [*Resto||Balance] On the first pull you can stand on top of hut to dodge <Wowhead type="npc" id="209801"/>'s !426893|Bounding Void!
- <Wowhead type="npc" id="207198"/>'s !430109|Lightning Bolt! can "one shot" if multiple are cast on the same person. Watch your health, use interrupts, defensive cooldowns and heals carefully.
- [*DPS||Resto] <Wowhead type="npc" id="214421"/>: Use defensive cooldowns for !430812|Attracting Shadows! if <Wowhead type="npc" id="214419"/> is leaping on you with !423979|Implosion! and you don't have !Ursol's Vortex!.
- <Wowhead type="npc" id="214419"/>: You can use !Ursol's Vortex! to stop !423979|Implosion!
- [*DPS||Resto] <Wowhead type="npc" id="212739"/>: Use defensive cooldowns on !472764|Void Extraction!.
</BossCard>

<BossCard title="Kyrioss" id="209230">
- [*Balance] Try to pool AP for movement during !444250|Lightning Torrent!/
- [*DPS||Resto] On higher key levels consider using !Bear Form! during !1214325|Crashing Thunder!.
- After the boss, you will get a feather. You can leap towards the birds in the cubbies and get the !434696|Stormrook's Favor! buff for 5 minutes.
</BossCard>

<BossCard title="Stormguard Gorren" id="207205">
- Getting more than 1 stack of !424739|Chaotic Corruption! is basically death. If you have the orb - stand next to a person than didn't get the buff yet.
- Use a defensive if you're affected by !424739|Chaotic Corruption! whilst the boss is casting !424958|Crush Reality!
</BossCard>

<BossCard title="Voidstone Monstrosity" id="207207">
- Try to drop !433067|Seeping Corruption! near the edges of the room.
- [*DPS] When !445262|Void Shell! is removed the boss will take 200% increased damage. You should be saving your cooldowns for the amps.
- [*Resto] Aim to position yourself in the middle of the room so that your heals can reach all the players.
- Use a defensive cooldown if you are affected by !433067|Seeping Corruption! whilst the boss is casting !423305|Null Upheaval!
</BossCard>
