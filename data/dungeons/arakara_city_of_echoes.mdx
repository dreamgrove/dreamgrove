<BossCard title="Trash">
- You can use !Incapacitating Roar! to stop <Wowhead type="npc" id="216340"/>'s !432967|Allarm Shrill! 
- [*Resto] <Wowhead type="npc" id="214840"/>'s !438618|Venomous Spit! should be cleansed with !Improved Nature's Cure!
- [*Resto] <Wowhead type="npc" id="218324"/>'s !438877|Call of the Brood! will do heavy AoE damage. Make sure your HOTs are out.
- [*Resto] <Wowhead type="npc" id="216365"/>s will use !433821|Dashing Strike! at random party members. You can use !Ursol's Vortex! to stop some of the hits.
</BossCard>

<BossCard title="Avanoxx" id="213179">
- Use !Barkskin! during !438476|Alerting Shrill!.
- Use !Ursol's Vortex! to help with the adds.
- [*Resto] !438476|Alerting Shrill! and !438960|Gossamer Onslaught! are the two main damage sources during the fight. The first hits more than the second.
- [*Resto] !438960|Gossamer Onslaught! provides no serious danger and can be healed almost passively with treants.
</BossCard>

<BossCard title="Anub'zekt" id="215405">
- You can use !Dash!, !Wild Charge! and !Stampeding Roar! to avoid !433731|Burrow Charge!
- [*Resto] !433740|Infestation! will do heavy ST damage. Consider moving !Lifebloom! to whoever is hit.
</BossCard>

<BossCard title="Ki'katal the Harvester" id="215407">
- You can wildshape to any form to free yourself from the roots. 
- !Improved Nature's Cure! or !Remove Corruption! can be used to dispel !461487|Cultivated Poisons!. Be careful not to dispel other people since they might run in their own puddles.
- [*Resto] Be careful if playing !Reforestation! or !Incarnation: Tree of Life! since shifting in or out of tree will break the roots.
</BossCard>
