## Playstyle

The forced tank swap on Mekkatorque is {{< spell 289699 "Electroshock Amplification" >}}, which stacks when he hits the same target. You should swap at around 8-10 stacks of this debuff.

**Catweavable**: Yes.

## Tips and Tricks

Use {{< spell 106898 "Stampeding Roar" >}} during {{< spell 287952 "Wormhole Generator" >}} to help the players with {{< spell 286646 "Gigavolt Charge" >}} to get into cover and break line of sight before their debuffs expire.
